Meenah's Portrait

Where Tradition Meets Art

Welcome to Meenahs Henna Art — a celebration of an ancient craft reimagined for modern beauty. Founded by Meenah, a passionate henna artist with roots in traditional Eastern artistry, our studio has been adorning hands and hearts for over a decade.

Every stroke is intentional. Every pattern tells a story. Whether it's a grand bridal occasion or a casual adornment, we bring the same dedication and love to every session.

We offer both local and black henna, specialising in intricate bridal sets that transform your hands and feet into living canvases.
